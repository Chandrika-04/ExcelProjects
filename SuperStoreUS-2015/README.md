# 🏪 SuperStore Financial Report — Microsoft Excel

A professional financial analytics case study built on real US retail transactional data — modelling a full Profit & Loss report, flagging KPIs with conditional formatting, analysing regional performance, and mapping a strategic FY 2020 project timeline.

## ✨ Key Features
- Financial Report (P&L) — departmental revenue, COGS, gross profit, operating expenses, and net margin with blue/black colour-coding convention
- Conditional Formatting — 7 rules auto-flagging profits (green), losses (red), return statuses, order priorities, and revenue scales
- Pivot Summary — 3 PivotTables covering expenses by department, revenue & returns by region, and monthly revenue trend with an interactive slicer
- Dashboard — 5 charts covering net profit by department, monthly revenue trend, revenue share by region, top 10 products by gross profit, and COGS vs gross profit scatter
- Gantt Chart — FY 2020 project timeline with 8+ milestones, colour-coded status (Completed / In Progress / Planned / Critical), and frozen columns
- Executive Summary — 400–600 word financial narrative with departmental highlights, regional analysis, risk flags, and 3+ strategic recommendations for FY 2020

## 🧰 Excel Concepts Used
`VLOOKUP` · `XLOOKUP` · `IFERROR` · `SUMIF` · `COUNTIF` · `AVERAGEIF` · `TEXT` · `Conditional Formatting` · `Color Scales` · `PivotTables` · `PivotChart` · `Slicers` · `Gantt via CF Grid` · `$#,##0;($#,##0);"-" Format`

## 📁 Sheets
